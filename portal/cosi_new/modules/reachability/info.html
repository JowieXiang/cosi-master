Erreichbarkeit:

Erreichbarkeit determines reachability areas from any given location on the map.

Parameter-Einstellungen:

Referenzpunkt - origin point of navigation

Transportmittel - transport means

Zeit(in min) - travel time

Ergebnisse:

The result will be displayed on the map as contours of polygons.

Please be reminded:

This service has a access limit, both in amount and frequency.

So please do not make unnecessary requests, or make requests too often.

In case of malfunction, please refresh the page and try again.
